LG SmartHome Developers Privacy Policy

(“LGE” or “we”) is committed to protecting the information of those who access and use the LG SmartHome Developers website (“Website”), the services that are made available on the Website (together with the Website, collectively referred to as “Services”), and one or more products associated with the Services (“Product”). Please be advised that LGE reserves the right to modify the Services at any time without notice.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains the types of information that we collect as a result of your use of the Services and how we use such information, including whether we share such information with a third party. To use the Services, you must create and hold a valid LG Account. Creation of the LG Account requires you to agree to the LG Account Privacy Policy; to the extent that there are any inconsistencies between the LG Account Privacy Policy and this Privacy Policy, this Privacy Policy will prevail as to your use of the Services.

2. What information does LGE obtain?

The information that we collect from your use of the Services falls into the following categories: • Device Information. We automatically collect information about your Products that you use with the Services. Device Information may include information to authenticate your Product with our servers such as Product code or Model code, your IP Address, type or nickname of your device, F/W and S/W Information, country code or device id(MAC address, AUID, UUID, etc.). We may also collect information about your connected device (e.g. smart phone, tablet, etc.) such as country code, language, OS, Time zone, phone number, network, memory capacity, etc. • Account Information. When you first create the LG Account, you will be asked to enter certain information, such as your email address (which will be your user ID), password, name, telephone number, date of birth, company name, company email, and/or company address. • Usage Information. Usage Information refers to all information collected in connection with your use of the Products or Services, such as what action has been applied to the Products or Services; your registration details provided through the Services; or how you may be using our Website (e.g., clicking logs). • IP Address. When you visit the Website, we may automatically log your IP address (the unique address which identifies your computer on the internet) which is automatically recognized by our server. • Cookie. A cookie is a small text file that may be used to collect information about your activity on the Website. For example, when someone visits a page within the Website, a cookie is placed in the user’s machine (if the user accepts cookies) or is read if the user has visited the Website previously. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser, but if you do, you may not be able to take advantage of the personalized features enjoyed by other users of our Website. Some of the cookies we may use include flash cookies or Adobe cookies. Depending on your browser, these cookies may not be deleted automatically. Please check your browser settings to determine where these types of cookies are stored and how they may be deleted.

3. How will LGE use the information it obtains?

We use your information that we collect from your use of the Services primarily for the following purposes: • To deliver or provide the Services; • To alert you to special offers, updated information and other new Products or Services provided by us, or other third parties, or to forward promotional materials to you; • To complete a transaction or service requested by you; • To ensure the Website is relevant to your needs; • To help us create Services most relevant to you; • To notify you about a material change to this privacy policy or the Terms of Use Agreement, if necessary; • to analyze, review, and improve the Products or Services we offer; • to understand how customers are using the Products and Website; • to develop new Products and Services we offer; • to protect and defend our rights and property, including by way of legal proceedings; • to comply with regulatory requirements;

LGE may share personal and non-personal information that we collect from your use of the Services with our affiliates and subsidiaries around the world for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. The term ‘affiliates’ or ‘subsidiaries’ refers to an LG group company that uses LG name or that has common ownership or control with LGE such as LG CNS. We generally do not share with a third party the information we collect from your use of the Services. Nevertheless, under the following circumstances, we may share such information with the following entities: - Third Party Service Providers in Connection with Provision of Services. In order to provide you with and improve the Services; - Other Parties When Required by Law. As required by court order or other government or law enforcement authorities in order to comply with legal process or law; - Other Parties in Connection with Corporate Transactions. In connection with a corporate event, such as a merger or sale of all or part of LGE’s business (including that of a division or subsidiary), where LGE may need to disclose information to a potential buyer and its professional advisers; - Other Parties When it is Necessary to Protect Our Services. In order to protect and defend our rights and property, including by way of legal proceedings; - Other Parties When it is to Protect Any of Our Users’ Personal Safety or Property. In order to protect the personal safety or property of our users or the public.

5. Collection and Use of Information from Children under the Age of 14

Protecting the privacy of children is important to us. LGE does not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from anyone under the age of 14 or knowingly allow such persons to use the Service. If you are under the age of 14, please do not attempt to use the Service or provide LGE with your personal information. If you are 14 or older but under the age of 18, you must have your parent or legal guardian's permission to use or access the Service. If LGE learns that the personal information of someone under the age of 14 is in LGE’s possession, LGE will promptly delete such personal information.

6. Overseas Transfers

We, our affiliates, subsidiaries, and suppliers may use the information that we collect from your use of the Services (including your personal information) in countries other than where you are located in connection with providing you with our Services and any other purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. The data protection laws in many of these countries may not offer the same level of protection as those in the country where you are located. However, regardless of the country to which your information is transferred, we will take steps to ensure that such information will be afforded a reasonable level of protection. By agreeing to this Privacy Policy you expressly consent to LGE and our business associates and suppliers processing your data in any country in accordance with this Privacy Policy, including, without limitation, Republic of Korea and United States.

7. How long will LGE keep my personal Information?

We will take reasonable steps to make sure that we keep your personal information for as long as is necessary for us to provide you with the Service or other purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

8. Cookies and other tracking technologies

When you use the Service, LGE and our third party partners may use cookies – small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters – and other tracking technologies. LGE may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Service. Please note that if you delete, or choose not to accept, cookies, you may not be able to utilize certain features of the Service to their full potential. When you use the Service, third party may collect personal information about your online activities; these third parties may also collect personal information about your online activities across different websites and mobile applications over time. LGE is not responsible for this third party data collection.

From time to time, we may communicate with you via email or text message in connection with your use of the Services. For example, we may use your email address to confirm your request, to send you information about changes to our Services, and to send notices and other disclosures as required by law. Generally, users cannot opt-out of these communications, but they will be primarily informational in nature rather than promotional.

This Privacy Policy may be updated by LGE from time to time without notice. LGE will inform you of any material change, and you will be asked to accept such change. If you do not agree to accept the change to this Privacy Policy, your use of some of the Services may be restricted.
